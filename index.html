About me

Hi, I’m Luis Fernando Herbozo Contreras, PhD candidate in Engineering at The University of Sydney, specializing in Biomedical and Computer Engineering. My research focuses on bio-inspired AI for real-world applications, particularly in healthcare, with interests in continual edge AI, neuromorphic computing, and privacy-aware. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Biomedical Engineering from Tecnológico de Monterrey, where I graduated Summa Cum Laude. Outside academia, I enjoy exercising, tutoring and socializing (Team Beer/Coffee).
