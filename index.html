About me

Hi, I’m Luis Fernando Herbozo Contreras, a third-year PhD candidate in Computer and Biomedical Engineering at The University of Sydney. My research focuses on developing low-power, bio-inspired AI algorithms for real-world applications, particularly in healthcare. I'm passionate about advancing edge AI, neuromorphic computing, and privacy-aware machine learning for personalized medical solutions. I hold a Bachelor's in Biomedical Engineering from Tecnologico de Monterrey (ITESM), where I graduated Magna Cum Laude. Outside academia, I enjoy swing music and dance (Lindy Hop, Charleston, Collegiate Shag), and I’ve recently started learning the saxophone.
